# The Willow Well Site

Premium one-page website and lightweight discovery-call intake system for The Willow Well, a Durham-based Somatic Strategy practice founded by Lee Gray.

## Goal

Build a visually polished, mobile-responsive MVP landing page that helps qualified organizational buyers understand The Willow Well, trust Lee Gray, and book a discovery call.

This is not a full app in v1. It is a premium landing page with basic lead qualification and calendar-protection logic.

## Core Positioning

The Willow Well helps high-performing leaders and teams build clarity, capacity, and sustainable performance through Somatic Strategy.

## Primary Audience

Corporate People & Culture leaders, CHROs, and L&D directors.

## Secondary Audiences

ESO and accelerator directors, founder ecosystem leaders, and mission-driven nonprofit leaders.

## Core CTA

Book a Discovery Call

## MVP Principles

- Local first
- People first
- Tech second
- Beautiful before complicated
- Structured but not overbuilt
- Premium, not precious
- Wellness-aligned without looking like a spa
- No fake proof
- No public pricing in v1
- No payments until offer/pricing/cancellation rules are confirmed
- No client login or dashboard in v1

## Brand Guardrails

Avoid:
- Yoga studio aesthetic
- Spa language
- Therapy positioning
- Woo-woo wellness clichés
- Generic HR brochure design
- Stock mindfulness imagery
- Heavy automations before the business rules are clear

Use:
- Cream-dominant design
- Slate text
- Sage accents
- Restrained gold CTAs
- Atmospheric nature imagery
- Calm authority
- Corporate credibility
- Clear discovery-call flow

## Launch Target

MVP dev site first. Production domain only after DNS, email records, and basic copy/visual review are safe.
